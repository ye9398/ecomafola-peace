# EcoMafola Peace — Return & Refund Policy

At EcoMafola Peace, we take immense pride in the craftsmanship of our Pacific artisans. Because our products are handcrafted using natural materials, slight variations in color, texture, and size are part of their unique story and charm.

### 🚢 Shipping Information
- **Free Worldwide Shipping**: We are proud to offer FREE shipping on all orders over **$35**. 
- **Standard Shipping**: For orders under $35, a standard shipping fee of $4.99 applies.

## 1. Damaged or Defective Items (Arrival Protection)
We want your "Polynesian Soul" to arrive in perfect condition.
- **Reporting Period**: Please inspect your order upon reception. If an item is defective, damaged, or if you receive the wrong item, contact us within **48 hours** at `hello@ecomafola.com` with photos of the damage and the packaging.
- **Our Solution**: For verified shipping damage or structural defects, we operate an **"Exchange-First"** policy. We will ship a replacement to you at no additional cost. If a replacement is unavailable, a full refund will be issued.

## 2. Returns & Change of Mind
Due to the high cost of international shipping and the fragile nature of our artisan goods (mirrors, lighting), we do not currently accept returns for "change of mind."
- All sales of handcrafted goods are final once shipped.
- Please review product descriptions, dimensions, and images carefully before purchasing.

## 3. Shipping Issues
- **Non-Delivery**: If your tracking has not updated for 15+ days, please contact us. We will work with our logistics partners (CJ Dropshipping/US Express) to locate your order.
- **Lost Packages**: If a package is confirmed lost by the carrier, we will ship a replacement or issue a full refund.

## 4. How to Initiate a Claim
Email us at `hello@ecomafola.com` with:
1. Your Order Number (#XXXX)
2. Clear photos of the item and the shipping label/box
3. A brief description of the issue
